About us Services

SERVICES

Falconry Press services include communication consultancy and strategy, campaigns, advocacy, event moderation, content marketing, podcasting, copywriting (speeches, op eds, short and long form articles, scripts, reports, position papers, bids), video and more. Our work centres on sustainability: communicating policies and projects for a greener and more equitable world.

Falconry Press people are skilled and accomplished professionals committed to providing high-quality client service. We enjoy communicating on topics such as energy, environment, climate, nature, circular economy, agriculture, health, and sustainability, and delivering excellent work.

Strategy

Strategy helps you to optimise resources and reach the right people. Refresh and refocus: get in touch for expert consultancy and advice on communication strategy.

Advocacy

Advocacy creates positive change. Get help with articulating your position, communicating effectively, outreach and establishing alliances for a common cause.

Moderation

Set the right tone for your event: be it a major conference, webinar, workshop or a panel discussion. Our experts engage speakers and audiences for inclusive and constructive debate.

Press releases and speeches

Want to spread the word? We turn your bullet points into compelling narratives to make your case and catch the attention of the wider world.

Blogs and articles

Need a blog post or a think-piece to boost your profile? We help you to clarify your thoughts, get your facts straight and ensure that your story is read by the people that matter.

Reports, brochures, flyers

We work with you through concept, planning, drafting, design and layout to ensure that the final product is accurate, concise, good-looking - and a decent read to boot. What could be nicer!

One-stop copy shop

Feeling stuck somewhere in the writing process? Need a second opinion, or got a long document to clean up fast? The Falconry Press one-stop copy shop is here to help.

Social media

Ready to communicate, connect and share information? We ensure that your social media strategy is aligned with your goals, adapted to current trends and suited to your audience.

Video

Looking for an eye-catching clip to enrich your social media, or a corporate video for your event? We produce and edit the most appropriate format and style for your needs.

Podcasts

Want a podcast that broadcasts your message, tells your story and stays within your budget? Let’s plan, host, record and edit high-quality sounds, publish and promote!

About us

Falconry Press is a boutique agency of seasoned communications professionals based in Brussels and providing a range of creative and professional services to big and small clients to help them meet their goals. We work in the fields of energy, environment, climate, nature, circular economy, agriculture, health, and sustainability.
